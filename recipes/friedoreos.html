Fried Oreos

Description

Deep Fried Oreos are an easy and fun treat for special occasions! Only a few ingredients are needed to make the batter crusted, powdered sugar dusted sweet indulgence!

Ingredients

  • Oreo cookies
  • Bisquick
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Vegetable oil
  • Powdered Sugar

Steps

  1. Whisk the batter ingredients together until smooth, in a medium-large bowl
  2. Dip one Oreo at a time into the batter to coat, and using a fork, remove the cookie from the batter, tapping off excess batter. You don't want all the batter to drip off, but any excess just tap lightly
  3. Place the Oreo directly into oil, heated up to 350°F in a deep Dutch oven. You want the oil to be at least 3- inches deep in the pot for a true “deep fry”. It just makes the process easier than going skimpy on the oil
  4. Allow the Oreos to fry for 3-ish minutes on each side until they are golden brown
  5. Transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate to cool
  6. Once they are no longer hot you can dust them with powdered sugar
