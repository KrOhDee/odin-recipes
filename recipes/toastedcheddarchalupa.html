Toasted Cheddar Chalupa

Description

Let's make a toasted cheddar chalupa!

A toasted cheddar chalupa is a chalupa shell with 6-month aged cheddar toasted on the outside and with seasoned beef, reduced-fat sour cream, tomatoes, lettuce, and cheddar cheese on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable shortening
  • 1 cup milk
  • Oil
  • Shredded Cheese
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Chopped tomato
  • Sour cream
  • Shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Combine flour, salt, and baking powder
  2. Work vegetable shortening in with fingers
  3. Add 3/4ths of milk into the flour and knead until solid dough forms
  4. Separate dough into 8 equal round balls and let the dough rest for 30 minutes
  5. Cook the ground beef and seasoning in a large skillet
  6. Heat up frying pan with 1-2 inches of vegetable oil on medium heat
  7. Roll out the dough into 6" discs and use a fork to poke holes in the dough
  8. Drop the dough in the oil and fry on both sides and fold
  9. On low heat sprinkle a tablespoon of shredded cheese in the pan and lay one side of the shell on top, repeat with the other side
  10. Stuff with ground beef, tomatoes, cheese, lettuce, and sour cream
